SkillBridge Platform Rules
1. One account per person. Multiple accounts = permanent ban + forfeited credits.
2. No fake services. List only skills you can genuinely deliver.
3. Escrow protects everyone. Credits are held until both parties confirm completion.
4. Reviews must be honest. Fake reviews = 30-day ban + 50 credit penalty.
5. Disputes are handled fairly. Submit evidence. Moderators decide within 48hrs.
6. No off-platform payments. All transactions must go through the escrow system.
7. Platform fee: 5% on all transactions (burned, not recycled, to prevent inflation).
8. Tax responsibility: You are responsible for reporting income in your jurisdiction.
9. Earning limit: 500 cr/day to prevent farming. Resets at midnight UTC.
10. Professional services (legal, medical, financial) require certification upload.
